About TourGether

Welcome to TourGether, the web app to simplify group trip planning.

TourGether was developed as part of May 2024's TechUp - a month long programme aimed at equipping Smart Nation (SN) officers with baseline technical skills so that officers can effectivel apply technology to solve real-world problem.

Read on the next few sections to find out more about what problem the webapp is trying to solve.

The Problem

Traveling with a group can be a thrilling experience, but it often comes with its fair share of challenges. One of the most common issues is the difficulty in planning and agreeing on a shared itinerary.

Different preferences and expectations among group members can lead to disagreements and frustrations, ultimately detracting from the overall trip experience.

The WebApp "Solution"

TourGether is designed to simplify and streamline the group decision-making process, making trip planning a breeze. A list of suggested destinations and activities will be generated based on the location and duration of your travel.

Members of the group can then vote on their preferences, and the app will consolidate all of your responses to generate a list of collectively decided places.

Key Features*

*Work in Progess

  1. GenAI Suggestions: Our AI-powered engine suggests the top destinations and activities tailored to your travel parameters.
  2. Group Voting: Members can vote on their preferred options, facilitating collaborative decision-making.
  3. Shared Scheduler/Planner: Once decisions are made, activities can be added to a shared scheduler/planner for easy organization and coordination.
