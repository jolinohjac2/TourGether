Results for 1 Day in SINGAPORE (3pax)

The number of places your group can visit in Singapore in a day depends on various factors such as the locations chosen, their proximity to each other, the mode of transport, time spent at each location and the group's pace.

As Singapore is a relatively small country with efficient public transportation, it is possible to cover 3-5 places in 1 day. Here are some suggestions (note: the list of places were not ranked in any particular order but a random order for variety):
